Oncomine Myeloid Panel - MyeloSEQer

Overview of the Assay

The NYU Onconmine Myeloid Assay is a comprehensive, targeted NGS assay using Ion AmpliSeq library Kit and Torrent sequencing platform (ThermoFisherTM). This assay can detect sequence variants in 50 genes (16 full genes and 22 hotspot genes), along with additional coverage of 17 fusion driver genes (Table 1) that have been previously identified as drivers of hematological malignancies.
